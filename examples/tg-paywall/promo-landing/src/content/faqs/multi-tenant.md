  Yes! The architecture supports **Variant C: SaaS with Custom Tokens**:
  
  - **Single codebase** runs on your server
  - **Multiple bot tokens** - each client/expert connects their own Telegram bot
  - **Unified database** - all subscriptions managed centrally
  - **White-label experience** - users see the expert's branded bot, not yours
  
  This model allows you to:
  - Sell the service to unlimited clients
  - Charge monthly subscription or revenue share
  - Maintain full control over the infrastructure
  - Let clients keep their brand identity
